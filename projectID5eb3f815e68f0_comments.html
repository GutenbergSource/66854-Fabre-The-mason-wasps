This is the eleventh book by Jean-Henri Fabre I release for proofreading in this "Fabre Flood" of books on insects. Most of these books are translations from his Souvenirs Entomologiques, however, not in the original order of chapters, but grouped by subject, and also includes some overlap of parts already published elsewhere in the series (or in other translations).

The complete set makes an impressively detailed popular treatise of the lives of insects.

The text itself is fairly straightforward to proof, so have fun!

The original scans are in the Internet Archive.
